THE REALITY

When we founded Oxygen Alliance in 2021, this community rallied around a single, urgent crisis. The pandemic exposed a lethal gap in global healthcare; we stepped in to ensure functional medical oxygen reached the patients who needed it most. We delivered.

But working alongside biomedical engineers in hospitals across low- and middle-income countries revealed a reality we could not ignore. Oxygen was just one piece of the puzzle. Today, up to 70% of all medical equipment in sub-Saharan Africa sits out of service. Monitors are dark. Incubators are cold.

Every patient deserves functional, safe medical equipment. We realized that to truly support these health systems, our mission had to expand. We are no longer just responding to an emergency; we are orchestrating a permanent transformation of the entire biomedical infrastructure.
